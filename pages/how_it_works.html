How It Works

The Magento Email Template Builer is an online app that allows design email templates for the Magento ecommerce system. With this app you can design your own custom style for your transactional emails by few click. Simple to use & professional design for emails. That's the power of the editor.


How?

  1. Choose your prefered design
  2. Choose language of your emails
  3. Edit email:

    4. In left part of the app you can play with the wysiwyg editor. Customize what you want (Colors, Images, Texts, Fonts etc.) for unique design of your store.
    In the area of emails you can overwrite links, texts etc.
    In the area among the editor and area of email, you can toggle emails. To see how they look after editing.

  4. Click at the download button
  5. Install the emails
  6. Done!

Make the emails looking beautiful just like rest of your online store using our solution.
